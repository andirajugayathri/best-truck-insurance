What Is Food Trailer Insurance?

Food trailer insurance is a specialised cover designed for mobile food operators who trade from a towable trailer instead of a traditional shopfront. It protects your trailer, your equipment, and your business operations from the everyday risks that come with working on the road.

This type of insurance steps in when unexpected events threaten your ability to trade whether it’s an accident while towing, a fire in your cooking area, an electrical fault that spoils your stock, or a claim made by a third party. With the right cover, you’re protected against repair costs, replacements, liability claims, and the financial shock of sudden downtime.

In short:

It keeps your business moving, no matter where you set up either markets, festivals, roadside spots, private events, or job sites.

What Does Food Trailer Insurance Cover?

A comprehensive policy typically includes:

Trailer Damage & Loss

Protection against collisions, rollovers, storms, fire, theft, and vandalism whether you're parked or on the road.

Public & Products Liability

Essential if someone is injured around your trailer or gets sick due to the food you serve.
This is often required by event organisers, councils, and markets.

Equipment, Appliances & Fit-Out

Covers fridges, fryers, grills, coffee machines, POS systems, signage, electrical fit-outs, and more (both fixed and portable).

Food & Stock Spoilage

If your power fails, equipment breaks down, or your trailer is in an accident, your perishable stock is protected.

Money & Portable Tools

Cover for stolen cash, EFTPOS terminals, tools, and mobile equipment.

Business Interruption

Helps you stay afloat financially if your trailer is damaged and you’re unable to trade temporarily.
