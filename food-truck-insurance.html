Overview

What is Food Truck Insurance?

Food Truck Insurance is a tailored set of commercial insurance products designed to protect a mobile food business from risks such as accidental damage to the truck, public liability claims, food-related incidents, equipment damage, or business downtime.

Unlike a standard car policy, food trucks require protection for both the vehicle and the food operation taking place inside it. Councils, markets, festivals, private sites, and franchisors regularly require liability cover before letting you trade.

With us, you get support from Food Truck Insurance Specialists Australia who understand mobility, downtime, regulations, and event requirements.

Every policy has limitations, and it is important to be clear upfront. Common exclusions may include:

  • Wear and tear or gradual deterioration
  • Mechanical breakdown unless listed
  • Unlicensed or intoxicated drivers
  • Unapproved modifications
  • Unregistered vehicles
  • Damage outside policy conditions
  • Loss caused by illegal activities
