What Is Mobile Catering Van Insurance?

Mobile catering van insurance is a specialised insurance package built for food trucks, coffee vans, ice-cream vans, barbecue trailers, donut vans, and every other mobile food setup. Unlike standard commercial vehicle insurance, this cover extends to the things that actually keep your business running.

Cooking equipment, refrigeration units, stock, fit-outs, and even your liability risks.

In simple terms:

It protects your wheels, your gear, your livelihood, and your peace of mind.

What's Covered?

Here's what a good mobile catering van insurance package should include:

Commercial Vehicle Cover

Protects your catering van for:

Accidents
Theft
Fire
Storm damage
Vandalism

Whether you're driving to a festival or parked at your usual spot, your van stays protected.

Equipment & Fit-Out Cover

Catering vans are full of costly gear. Your cover can include:

Coffee machines
Gas burners
Hot plates
Refrigeration units
Grills & fryers
Stainless-steel fit-outs
Generators

If it's fixed to your van or essential for service, it can be insured.

Public & Products Liability

Absolutely essential for mobile food operators. Covers you if:

A customer burns themselves
Someone slips near your service area
A customer becomes sick from your food
Your equipment damages someone's property

Many councils, markets, and events won't even let you trade without this cover.

Stock & Contents Cover

Protects your:

Ingredients
Packaging
Perishables
Consumables

This is especially useful if fridges fail, spoilage occurs, or fire damage ruins your stock.

Business Interruption

If your van is off the road due to a covered event, this helps protect your income until you're back up and running.
