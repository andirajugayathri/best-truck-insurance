What is tow truck insurance?

Tow truck insurance is a specialized form of commercial truck insurance which helps protect your truck from many risks on the road. This insurance is vital in protecting your business's livelihood.

Why do you need tow truck insurance?

This is a way to protect your assets against possible disasters, damages, and lawsuits that could happen in the event of a loss. Without this, your business could face more operational difficulties or legal problems.

What is a tow truck?

A tow truck (also called a wrecker, a breakdown truck, a recovery vehicle, or a breakdown lorry), is a truck used to move disabled, improperly parked, impounded, or otherwise indisposed motor vehicles.

The main types of tow trucks are:

Wheel Lift tow Trucks
Hook and Chain Trucks
Integrated Tow Trucks
Flatbed

On hook liability protects the vehicle hooked up to a tow truck (using hooks tilt-tray or jib and boom mechanisms). This insurance will cover the cost of repair or replacement if any damage occurs to the car which is not yours during transit, for the purpose of collection or delivery of the vehicle within a 200 meter radius of the tow truck.

You can choose from the following Hook Liability Limits: $100,000 / $250,000 / $500,000 / 750,000 / $1,000,000

Marine

This cover is designed to cover items not insured under the hook liability e.g. machinery on the tray and is covered Australia wide. This covers items that are lost, stolen, or damaged in transit.

You can choose one of the following limits: $100,000 / $250,000 / $500,000

Public Liability

This insurance protects you in the event that you are liable to a third party that has sustained personal, business, or property damage as a result of your business activities. In case of others take legal action against you, it also covers legal defence costs. We can provide a minimum cover of $10M.

Business Interruption

This cover is designed to get you and your business back up and running after an event or damage and will pay your loss of income whilst your vehicle is being repaired or replaced.
