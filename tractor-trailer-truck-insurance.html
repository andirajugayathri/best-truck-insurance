What Is Tractor Trailer Truck Insurance?

Tractor Trailer Truck Insurance is a specialised commercial motor insurance designed for truck or prime movers and attached trailers used for freight transport in Australia. It covers your tractor unit, trailer, cargo, and liability in case something goes wrong on the road or at the depot.

This type of policy is tailored for:

  • Owner-drivers
  • Fleet operators
  • Long-haul and interstate carriers
  • Local and regional freight operators
  • Logistics and transport companies

It’s not the same as normal commercial vehicle insurance. Tractor-trailers face higher risks, heavier loads, and longer travel distances, so they need a dedicated policy.

Why Is Tractor Trailer Insurance Mandatory?

Having proper insurance is not just recommended, it’s essential.

Legal requirement

Public Liability and Compulsory Third Party (CTP) cover are requirements in every Australian state and territory.

Finance and lease obligation

If your tractor or trailer is financed, lenders require comprehensive cover.

Protection from legal claims

If your rig causes damage or injury, the costs can reach hundreds of thousands sometimes millions.

Industry compliance

Major transport clients, warehouses, ports and distribution centres demand proof of insurance before allowing access.

In short: no insurance = no work.

What Does Tractor Trailer Truck Insurance Cover?

Coverage can be tailored, but most operators choose a comprehensive package that includes:

Tractor / Truck (Prime Mover) Cover

Protection against:

  • Accidents
  • Rollovers
  • Fire
  • Theft
  • Vandalism
  • Storm & natural events
  • Towing & recovery

Trailer Cover

Covers damage or loss to:

  • Flatbeds
  • Refrigerated trailers
  • Curtain-siders
  • Drop-decks
  • Low-loaders
  • Tankers

Goods in Transit / Cargo Cover

Protects customer freight from:

  • Accidental damage
  • Wet damage
  • Loading/unloading incidents
  • Theft during transit
  • Temperature loss for refrigerated loads

Liability Cover

Protection if your truck causes:

  • Vehicle damage
  • Property damage
  • Load spills
  • Clean-up expenses
  • Injury to 3rd parties

Downtime Cover

Pays you a daily benefit when your truck is off the road after an insured event, keeping your cashflow moving.
